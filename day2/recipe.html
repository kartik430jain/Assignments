Paneer Lababdar Recipe

    Ingredients

  1. 50 grams paneer, cubed
  2. 1 large onion, finely chopped
  3. 2 tomatoes, finely chopped
  4. 1 green chili, finely chopped (optional)
  5. 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  6. 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  7. 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  8. 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  9. 1/2 teaspoon garam masala powder
  10. 1/4 cup fresh cream
  11. 2 tablespoons oil
  12. Salt to taste
  13. Chopped coriander leaves for garnishing

    Cooking Steps

  • Prepare the Cashew Paste
  • Fry the Paneer
  • Prepare the Gravy
  • Add Tomato and Spice Mix
  • Make the Cashew Paste
  • Add Paneer and Cream
  • Finish with Kasuri Methi and Garam Masala
  • Garnish and Serve
