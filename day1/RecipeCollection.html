    • Recipe Of Casatta Pastry:

    • 1 cup granulated sugar
    • 1 cup all-purpose flour
    • 1 teaspoon baking powder

      For the Filling:

    • 1/2 cup chopped candied fruit (such as cherries, orange peel, citron)

    Main course

    • Dal Makhani

      • Recipe Of Dal Makhani:

      • Rinse the black lentils (urad dal) and kidney beans (rajma) under cold water until the water runs clear. Soak them in water for at least 6 hours or overnight.
      • After soaking, drain the water from the lentils and beans. Transfer them to a pressure cooker along with 4 cups of fresh water. Cook on medium heat for about 15-20 minutes until they are soft and fully cooked. You can also use a slow cooker or cook them in a pot on the stove, but it will take longer.
      • In a separate pan, heat ghee or butter over medium heat. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter. Add chopped onions to the pan and sauté until they turn golden brown. Add ginger-garlic paste and slit green chili (if using). Sauté for another 2 minutes until the raw smell disappears.

    • Paneer Lababdar

      • Recipe Of Paneer Lababdar:

      • Heat 2 tablespoons of oil in a pan. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter. Add finely chopped onions and green chilies to the pan. Sauté until the onions turn golden brown. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for another 2 minutes until the raw smell disappears.Add tomato puree to the pan and cook until the oil starts to separate from the mixture.
      • Add red chili powder, turmeric powder, coriander powder, garam masala, and salt to the tomato mixture. Mix well and cook for another 2-3 minutes. Add fresh cream to the mixture and stir until well combined. Cook for 2 more minutes and then turn off the heat. This is your paneer masala mixture. In a separate pan, heat 2 tablespoons of oil or ghee. Add finely chopped onions and sauté until they turn translucent. Add finely chopped tomatoes and ginger-garlic paste to the pan. Cook until the tomatoes are soft and mushy.
      • Finally, add fresh cream to the paneer lababdar and mix well. Cook for another minute.Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with naan, roti, or rice. Enjoy your delicious Paneer Lababdar.
