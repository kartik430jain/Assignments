Tips

    Tip 1

  • This iconic palace complex is a must-visit in Udaipur. Explore its ornate courtyards, beautiful gardens, and impressive architecture that offer breathtaking views of the city and Lake Pichola.

    • Tip 2

  • Wander through the narrow lanes of Udaipur's old city, filled with colorful markets, bustling bazaars, and charming cafes. Don't miss the vibrant atmosphere of the local markets, where you can shop for traditional Rajasthani handicrafts, textiles, and souvenirs.
